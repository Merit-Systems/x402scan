# Contact Enrichment

Transform basic contact information into complete, verified profiles ready for outreach.

---

## Enrich from Company Domain

Find the right contacts when you only know the company.

```prompt
---
title: Domain-Based Search
description: Find key contacts at a company from their domain
icon: globe
---
I want to reach out to {{Domain|cloudflare.com}}. Find me:
- The {{Role 1|VP of Sales or CRO}} with their email
- The {{Role 2|Head of Partnerships}} with their email
- Any relevant decision makers in {{Department|business development}}
```

---

## Bulk Enrich a Lead List

Process an existing list with missing contact information.

```prompt
---
title: Bulk Lead Enrichment
description: Enrich a list of leads with missing contact information
icon: list
---
I have a CSV of {{Count|50}} leads with name and company. Enrich all of them with verified email addresses, phone numbers, and LinkedIn URLs. Flag any that couldn't be found.
```


---

## Verify Email Deliverability

Check if emails are valid before sending campaigns.

```prompt
---
title: Email Verification
description: Verify email addresses before sending campaigns
icon: mail-check
---
I have these {{Count|10}} email addresses from an old list. Verify which ones are still valid and get updated contact info for any that bounced.
```


---

## Find Personal Contact Info

When corporate emails bounce, find alternative contact methods.

```prompt
---
title: Personal Contact Finder
description: Find personal contact info when corporate emails fail
icon: user-search
---
I can't reach {{Email|john.smith@acme.com}} - it's bouncing. Find his personal email or mobile number so I can reach him directly.
```
