# Account Research

Gather comprehensive intelligence on target accounts to personalize outreach and identify opportunities.

---

## Company Deep Dive

Get a complete picture of a target account before a meeting.

```prompt
---
title: Account Overview
description: Get a complete picture of a target account
icon: building
---
Research {{Company|Acme Corp}} thoroughly. I need:
- Company overview (size, funding, revenue if available)
- Leadership team and their backgrounds
- Recent news and announcements
- Tech stack and tools they use
- Any recent job postings that signal priorities
```

**APIs Used:** Apollo Org Enrich → Apollo People Search → Serper News → Exa Search → Firecrawl Scrape (careers page)

---

## Map the Buying Committee

Identify all stakeholders involved in purchasing decisions.

```prompt
---
title: Buying Committee
description: Map all stakeholders involved in purchasing decisions
icon: users
---
I'm selling to {{Company|Acme Corp}}'s {{Team|engineering}} team. Map out everyone who might be involved in a purchasing decision:
- Technical evaluators (senior engineers, architects)
- Budget holders (VP/Director level)
- Executive sponsors (CTO, CEO)

Include their LinkedIn and email for each.
```

**APIs Used:** Apollo People Search (multiple queries by role) → Apollo People Enrich

---

## Track Company News and Signals

Monitor for buying signals and conversation starters.

```prompt
---
title: Signal Tracking
description: Monitor a company for buying signals and news
icon: bell
---
What's been happening at {{Company|Datadog}} in the last {{Timeframe|month}}? Find:
- Press releases and announcements
- Executive interviews or podcasts
- Product launches
- Hiring patterns that indicate priorities
```

**APIs Used:** Serper News → Grok X Search → Exa Search

---

## Analyze Competitive Displacement Opportunities

Find accounts using competitor products.

```prompt
---
title: Displacement Opportunities
description: Find companies using competitor products
icon: shuffle
---
Find companies that mention using {{Competitor|Competitor X}} on their engineering blog, job postings, or Stack Overflow discussions. These are potential displacement opportunities.
```

**APIs Used:** Exa Search → Firecrawl Scrape → Apollo Org Enrich
