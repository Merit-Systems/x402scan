# Recruiting Workflows

Automate your candidate sourcing, enrichment, and competitive intelligence with AI-powered research.

## What you can do

- **Source Candidates** - Find qualified candidates using Apollo and LinkedIn data based on role, location, and company criteria
- **Enrich Profiles** - Transform basic candidate info into comprehensive profiles with contact details and work history
- **Map Competitors** - Build organizational charts and track talent movement at target companies
