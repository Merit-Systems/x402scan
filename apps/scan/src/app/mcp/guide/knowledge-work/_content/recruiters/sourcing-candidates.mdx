## Find Engineers at Specific Companies

Search for software engineers at target companies by domain or organization ID.

```
Find 20 senior software engineers at Stripe. Include their LinkedIn URLs,
current titles, and email addresses. Focus on those with backend or
infrastructure experience.
```

**APIs Used:** Apollo People Search → Apollo People Enrich

---

## Source Candidates by Skills and Location

Build a candidate pool based on job title, seniority, and geography.

```
Find ML engineers in the San Francisco Bay Area who are currently at
Series A-C startups (50-500 employees). I need their contact info and
employment history.
```

**APIs Used:** Apollo People Search → Apollo People Enrich → Clado LinkedIn Scrape (for detailed profiles)

---

## Find Passive Candidates via Social Signals

Identify candidates who are actively sharing content or engaging in industry discussions.

```
Search X/Twitter for people posting about distributed systems, Rust, or
Kubernetes who have "engineer" or "developer" in their bio. Then enrich
their profiles with Apollo to get professional details.
```

**APIs Used:** Grok User Search → Apollo People Enrich (by name + company)

---

## Map Talent at Competitor Companies

Build a comprehensive view of engineering talent at competing organizations.

```
Create a talent map of all engineering managers and directors at
Datadog, Splunk, and New Relic. Include their tenure, previous
companies, and LinkedIn profiles.
```

**APIs Used:** Apollo Org Search (verify IDs) → Apollo People Search → Apollo People Enrich
