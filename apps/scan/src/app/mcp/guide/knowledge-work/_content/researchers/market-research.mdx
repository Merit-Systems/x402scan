# Market Research

Conduct comprehensive market research using web search, news, and company data.

---

## Industry Landscape Overview

Get a comprehensive view of a market or industry.

```prompt
---
title: Industry Overview
description: Get a comprehensive view of a market or industry landscape
icon: globe
---
Research the {{Market|observability/monitoring}} market. I need:
- Key players and their market positioning
- Recent funding and M&A activity
- Market size estimates from analyst reports
- Emerging trends and technologies

Cite all sources.
```

---

## Identify Market Entrants

Track new companies entering a space.

```prompt
---
title: New Entrants
description: Find new companies entering a specific market
icon: log-in
---
Find all companies founded in the last {{Timeframe|2 years}} that are building {{Product Type|AI coding assistants or developer productivity tools}}. Include their funding, team size, and product focus.
```

---

## Analyze Market Sizing Data

Gather data points for TAM/SAM/SOM calculations.

```prompt
---
title: Market Sizing
description: Gather data for market sizing calculations
icon: pie-chart
---
Help me size the market for {{Product Category|API security tools}}:
- Find analyst reports with market size estimates
- Identify the number of potential customers (companies using APIs)
- Research pricing models of existing solutions
```

## Geographic Market Analysis

Research a market in a specific region.

```prompt
---
title: Regional Analysis
description: Analyze a market landscape in a specific geographic region
icon: map
---
What does the {{Industry|fintech}} landscape look like in {{Region|Southeast Asia}}?
- Major hubs and ecosystems
- Key companies and recent funding
- Regulatory environment
- Growth trends vs. other regions
```
