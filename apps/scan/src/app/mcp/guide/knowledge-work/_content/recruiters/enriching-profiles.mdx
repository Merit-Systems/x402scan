# Enriching Profiles

Transform basic candidate information into comprehensive profiles with contact details and work history.

---

## Enrich from LinkedIn URL

Get full professional details from a LinkedIn profile URL.

```prompt
---
title: LinkedIn Profile Enrichment
description: Get full professional details from a LinkedIn profile
icon: linkedin
---
I have this LinkedIn profile: {{LinkedIn URL|linkedin.com/in/example-candidate}}
Get their full work history, current email, phone number, and any other contact information available.
```

**APIs Used:** Clado LinkedIn Scrape → Clado Contacts Enrich (for personal email/phone)

---

## Bulk Enrich a Candidate List

Process multiple candidates at once when you have partial information.

```prompt
---
title: Bulk Candidate Enrichment
description: Enrich multiple candidates with names and companies
icon: users
---
I have a list of {{Count|10}} candidates with their names and current companies. Enrich all of them with email addresses and LinkedIn profiles:
- {{Candidate 1|Jane Smith, Acme Corp}}
- {{Candidate 2|John Doe, TechStartup Inc}}
[...]
```

**APIs Used:** Apollo People Enrich Bulk

---

## Fill in Missing Contact Info

When Apollo data is incomplete, fall back to Clado for personal contact details.

```prompt
---
title: Contact Info Fallback
description: Find personal contact details when corporate data is incomplete
icon: phone
---
I have this candidate's Apollo profile but need their personal email and phone number for outreach. Their LinkedIn is {{LinkedIn URL|linkedin.com/in/candidate}}
```

**APIs Used:** Clado Contacts Enrich (with email_enrichment and phone_enrichment flags)

---

## Verify and Update Stale Data

Re-enrich candidates whose information may be outdated.

```prompt
---
title: Data Refresh
description: Update stale candidate data with fresh information
icon: refresh-cw
---
This candidate's Apollo data shows they were at {{Old Company|Company X}} but their LinkedIn shows {{New Company|Company Y}}. Get me fresh data from their LinkedIn profile and update their contact information.
```

**APIs Used:** Clado LinkedIn Scrape → Apollo People Enrich (to cross-reference)
