# Company Due Diligence

Conduct thorough due diligence on companies before making investment decisions.

---

## Company Overview Research

Build a comprehensive company profile.

```prompt
---
title: Due Diligence Report
description: Create a comprehensive due diligence report on a target company
icon: file-text
---
Create a due diligence report on {{Company|Acme Corp}}:
- Company history and founding story
- Product and technology overview
- Business model and pricing
- Funding history and cap table info
- Team size and key hires
- Market positioning and competitors
```
