# Competitive Mapping

Build detailed organizational maps of engineering, product, and leadership teams at target companies.

---

## Map an Engineering Organization

Understand the structure and key players in a company's engineering team.

```prompt
---
title: Org Chart Mapping
description: Map the engineering organization structure at a target company
icon: sitemap
---
Map the engineering organization at {{Company|Figma}}. I want to understand:
- Engineering leadership (VP, Directors, Managers)
- Team structure and reporting lines where visible
- Key technical leaders and their focus areas
- Total engineering headcount estimate

Provide LinkedIn profiles for all leadership.
```

---

## Identify Recent Hires and Departures

Track talent movement at competitor companies.

```prompt
---
title: Talent Movement Tracker
description: Track recent hires and departures at a company
icon: arrow-right-left
---
Find people who joined {{Company|Notion}} in the last {{Timeframe|6 months}} in {{Department|engineering or product}} roles. Also identify any senior departures I can see from LinkedIn data showing short tenures.
```

---

## Compare Team Sizes Across Competitors

Benchmark headcount and team composition across multiple companies.

```prompt
---
title: Team Comparison
description: Compare team sizes and composition across competitors
icon: bar-chart
---
Compare the {{Team Type|data engineering}} teams at {{Company 1|Databricks}}, {{Company 2|Snowflake}}, and {{Company 3|Confluent}}. For each company, give me:
- Estimated team size
- Seniority distribution
- Key leaders with their backgrounds
```

## Find Acquisition Targets by Talent

Identify small companies with exceptional talent density.

```prompt
---
title: Talent Density Search
description: Find small companies with exceptional talent from top organizations
icon: gem
---
Find {{Stage|seed-stage}} {{Industry|AI/ML}} startups (under {{Size|50 employees}}) in {{Location|San Francisco}} that have hired engineers from {{Source Companies|Google Brain, DeepMind, or OpenAI}}. List the companies and the notable hires.
```
