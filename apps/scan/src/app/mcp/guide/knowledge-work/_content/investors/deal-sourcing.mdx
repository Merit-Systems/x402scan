# Deal Sourcing

Identify promising investment opportunities by discovering emerging companies and tracking market signals.

---

## Find Companies by Thesis

Source deals that match your investment thesis.

```prompt
---
title: Thesis-Based Search
description: Find early-stage companies matching your investment thesis
icon: target
---
Find early-stage companies ({{Stage|seed to Series A}}) working on {{Focus Area|AI infrastructure}} - specifically {{Specifics|model serving, fine-tuning, or evaluation tools}}. Include:
- Company name and website
- Founding team backgrounds
- Funding history
- Product description
```

---

## Track Funding Announcements

Monitor the funding landscape in your focus areas.

```prompt
---
title: Funding Tracker
description: Track recent funding announcements in your focus area
icon: trending-up
---
What {{Stage|seed and Series A}} rounds were announced in {{Industry|developer tools and infrastructure}} in the last {{Timeframe|30 days}}? Include the company, amount raised, and lead investors.
```

---

## Source via Talent Signals

Find companies attracting exceptional talent.

```prompt
---
title: Talent Signal Search
description: Find startups attracting exceptional talent from top companies
icon: user-check
---
Find startups that have recently hired engineers from:
- {{Company 1|Google (DeepMind, Brain, Core ML teams)}}
- {{Company 2|OpenAI}}
- {{Company 3|Anthropic}}

These talent moves often signal promising companies.
```

---

## Discover via Social Signals

Find founders building in public before they raise.

```prompt
---
title: Social Signal Discovery
description: Find founders building in public on social media
icon: message-circle
---
Search X/Twitter for founders and builders talking about:
- "We're building" + {{Topic|AI/ML/infrastructure}}
- Technical deep dives on novel approaches
- Early product launches and demos

Filter for accounts with technical credibility.
```

## Geographic Deal Sourcing

Find opportunities in specific regions or ecosystems.

```prompt
---
title: Geographic Search
description: Map the startup ecosystem in a specific region
icon: map-pin
---
Map the early-stage {{Industry|fintech}} ecosystem in {{Region|Latin America}}:
- Companies founded in the last {{Years|3 years}}
- Based in {{Countries|Brazil, Mexico, or Colombia}}
- {{Stage|Seed to Series A}} stage
- Founding team with relevant experience
```
