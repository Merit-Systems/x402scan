# Deal Sourcing

Identify promising investment opportunities by discovering emerging companies and tracking market signals.

---

## Find Companies by Thesis

Source deals that match your investment thesis.

```
Find early-stage companies (seed to Series A) working on AI
infrastructure - specifically model serving, fine-tuning, or
evaluation tools. Include:
- Company name and website
- Founding team backgrounds
- Funding history
- Product description
```

**APIs Used:** Exa Search → Apollo Org Enrich → Apollo People Search (founders) → Apollo People Enrich

---

## Track Funding Announcements

Monitor the funding landscape in your focus areas.

```
What seed and Series A rounds were announced in developer tools
and infrastructure in the last 30 days? Include the company,
amount raised, and lead investors.
```

**APIs Used:** Serper News → Exa Search → Apollo Org Enrich

---

## Source via Talent Signals

Find companies attracting exceptional talent.

```
Find startups that have recently hired engineers from:
- Google (DeepMind, Brain, Core ML teams)
- OpenAI
- Anthropic

These talent moves often signal promising companies.
```

**APIs Used:** Apollo People Search (ex-employees) → Apollo Org Enrich (current companies)

---

## Discover via Social Signals

Find founders building in public before they raise.

```
Search X/Twitter for founders and builders talking about:
- "We're building" + AI/ML/infrastructure
- Technical deep dives on novel approaches
- Early product launches and demos

Filter for accounts with technical credibility.
```

**APIs Used:** Grok X Search → Grok User Search → Apollo People Enrich (verify backgrounds)

---

## Geographic Deal Sourcing

Find opportunities in specific regions or ecosystems.

```
Map the early-stage fintech ecosystem in Latin America:
- Companies founded in the last 3 years
- Based in Brazil, Mexico, or Colombia
- Seed to Series A stage
- Founding team with relevant experience
```

**APIs Used:** Apollo Org Search → Apollo People Search → Exa Search (local tech news)
