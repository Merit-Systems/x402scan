# Trend Monitoring

Stay ahead of industry trends, emerging technologies, and market shifts.

---

## Track Emerging Technologies

Monitor the development and adoption of new technologies.

```prompt
---
title: Technology Tracker
description: Monitor the state of an emerging technology
icon: cpu
---
What's the current state of {{Technology|AI agents in production}}? Find:
- Companies shipping agent products
- Technical approaches being used
- Adoption patterns and use cases
- Key challenges and limitations
- Thought leaders in the space
```

**APIs Used:** Exa Search → Grok X Search → Reddit Search → Firecrawl Scrape

---

## Monitor Industry News

Set up comprehensive news monitoring for a topic.

```prompt
---
title: News Monitor
description: Gather recent news on a specific topic
icon: newspaper
---
Gather all significant news from the last {{Timeframe|week}} about:
- {{Topic 1|Large language model releases and updates}}
- {{Topic 2|AI regulation and policy changes}}
- {{Topic 3|Major funding rounds in AI}}
- {{Topic 4|Notable AI product launches}}
```

**APIs Used:** Serper News → Exa Search → Grok X Search

## Track Thought Leader Activity

Monitor what industry experts are saying.

```prompt
---
title: Thought Leader Monitor
description: Track what industry experts are discussing
icon: mic
---
What are the top {{Industry|AI}} researchers and founders saying on X/Twitter this {{Timeframe|week}}? Summarize the key themes and debates in the {{Community|AI engineering}} community.
```

**APIs Used:** Grok User Search → Grok User Posts → Grok X Search

## Identify Emerging Startups

Find early-stage companies working on emerging trends.

```prompt
---
title: Emerging Startup Search
description: Find early-stage startups in emerging areas
icon: rocket
---
Find {{Stage|seed-stage}} startups (founded {{Years|2023-2024}}) working on:
- {{Focus 1|AI infrastructure}}
- {{Focus 2|Developer tools}}
- {{Focus 3|Data engineering}}

Include their funding, team backgrounds, and what makes them notable.
```

**APIs Used:** Exa Search → Apollo Org Search → Apollo People Search (founders) → Apollo People Enrich

---

## Community Sentiment Analysis

Understand how practitioners feel about technologies or tools.

```prompt
---
title: Sentiment Analysis
description: Analyze community sentiment about a technology or tool
icon: heart
---
What's the developer sentiment around {{Technology|Technology X}}?
- Search Reddit for discussions and opinions
- Check X/Twitter for real-time reactions
- Find blog posts with honest reviews
- Identify common pain points and praise
```

**APIs Used:** Reddit Search → Reddit Post Comments → Grok X Search → Exa Search
