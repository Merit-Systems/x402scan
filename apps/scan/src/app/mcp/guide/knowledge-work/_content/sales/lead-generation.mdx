# Lead Generation

Build qualified prospect lists using company and people search with multi-signal enrichment.

---

## Find Decision Makers at Target Companies

Identify the right contacts at companies matching your ICP.

```
Find VP and Director-level contacts in Engineering and IT at
enterprise companies (1000+ employees) in financial services.
Include their email addresses and LinkedIn profiles.
```

**APIs Used:** Apollo People Search → Apollo People Enrich

---

## Build a List from Industry + Tech Stack

Target companies using specific technologies or in specific markets.

```
Find SaaS companies using Kubernetes that have raised Series B or
later. Then get me the CTO or VP Engineering at each company with
their contact information.
```

**APIs Used:** Exa Search (tech stack signals) → Apollo Org Search → Apollo People Search → Apollo People Enrich

---

## Source Leads from Recent Funding Announcements

Target companies with fresh capital who may be expanding.

```
Search for companies that announced Series A or B funding in the
last 30 days in the developer tools space. Get the founder/CEO
contact info for each.
```

**APIs Used:** Serper News → Apollo Org Enrich → Apollo People Search → Apollo People Enrich

---

## Find Companies by Location and Size

Build geographically targeted prospect lists.

```
Find all software companies with 50-200 employees in Austin, Texas.
Get me the Head of Sales or VP Sales at each with email and phone.
```

**APIs Used:** Apollo Org Search → Apollo People Search → Apollo People Enrich → Clado Contacts Enrich (fallback)
