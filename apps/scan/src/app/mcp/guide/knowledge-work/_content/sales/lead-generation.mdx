# Lead Generation

Build qualified prospect lists using company and people search with multi-signal enrichment.

---

## Find Decision Makers at Target Companies

Identify the right contacts at companies matching your ICP.

```prompt
---
title: Decision Maker Search
description: Find decision makers at companies matching your ICP
icon: target
---
Find {{Seniority|VP and Director-level}} contacts in {{Department|Engineering and IT}} at {{Company Type|enterprise companies (1000+ employees)}} in {{Industry|financial services}}. Include their email addresses and LinkedIn profiles.
```

**APIs Used:** Apollo People Search → Apollo People Enrich

---

## Build a List from Industry + Tech Stack

Target companies using specific technologies or in specific markets.

```prompt
---
title: Tech Stack Search
description: Find companies using specific technologies
icon: layers
---
Find {{Company Type|SaaS companies}} using {{Technology|Kubernetes}} that have raised {{Stage|Series B or later}}. Then get me the {{Role|CTO or VP Engineering}} at each company with their contact information.
```

**APIs Used:** Exa Search (tech stack signals) → Apollo Org Search → Apollo People Search → Apollo People Enrich

---

## Source Leads from Recent Funding Announcements

Target companies with fresh capital who may be expanding.

```prompt
---
title: Funding-Based Leads
description: Find companies with recent funding announcements
icon: trending-up
---
Search for companies that announced {{Stage|Series A or B}} funding in the last {{Timeframe|30 days}} in the {{Industry|developer tools}} space. Get the {{Role|founder/CEO}} contact info for each.
```

**APIs Used:** Serper News → Apollo Org Enrich → Apollo People Search → Apollo People Enrich

---

## Find Companies by Location and Size

Build geographically targeted prospect lists.

```prompt
---
title: Geographic Lead Search
description: Find companies by location and size
icon: map-pin
---
Find all {{Industry|software}} companies with {{Size|50-200 employees}} in {{Location|Austin, Texas}}. Get me the {{Role|Head of Sales or VP Sales}} at each with email and phone.
```

**APIs Used:** Apollo Org Search → Apollo People Search → Apollo People Enrich → Clado Contacts Enrich (fallback)
