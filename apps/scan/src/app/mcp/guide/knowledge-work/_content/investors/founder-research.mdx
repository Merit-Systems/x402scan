# Founder Research

Conduct deep diligence on founders, their backgrounds, and their track records.

---

## Founder Background Check

Get a comprehensive view of a founder's history.

```
Research the founders of [Company X]:
- Full career history and education
- Previous startups or exits
- Technical background and expertise
- Publications, talks, or open source contributions
- Social presence and thought leadership
```

**APIs Used:** Apollo People Enrich → Clado LinkedIn Scrape → Exa Search → Grok User Search → Grok User Posts

---

## Verify Claims and Credentials

Fact-check founder backgrounds and achievements.

```
A founder claims to be "ex-Google AI" and have worked on
[specific project]. Verify this claim by:
- Finding their LinkedIn employment history
- Searching for their name + Google + the project
- Looking for any publications or talks
```

**APIs Used:** Clado LinkedIn Scrape → Exa Search → Apollo People Enrich

---

## Map the Founding Team Network

Understand how founders know each other and their shared history.

```
Map the professional network of [Company X]'s founding team:
- Where did they work together previously?
- Do they have complementary skills?
- What's their history of collaboration?
```

**APIs Used:** Clado LinkedIn Scrape (all founders) → Apollo People Enrich

---

## Research Serial Founders

Track founders across multiple ventures.

```
Find all companies founded by [Person X]. For each company:
- When was it founded and what did it do?
- What happened (exit, shut down, still running)?
- Who were the co-founders and investors?
```

**APIs Used:** Apollo People Enrich → Exa Search → Serper News → Apollo Org Enrich

---

## Assess Technical Credibility

Evaluate a technical founder's depth.

```
Evaluate the technical credibility of [Founder Name] who's
building an ML infrastructure company:
- What's their publication record?
- Open source contributions?
- Technical blog posts or talks?
- Where did they work and on what?
```

**APIs Used:** Exa Search (academic/technical content) → Clado LinkedIn Scrape → Grok X Search
