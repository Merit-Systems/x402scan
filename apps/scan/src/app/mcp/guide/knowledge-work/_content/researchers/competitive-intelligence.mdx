# Competitive Intelligence

Monitor competitors, analyze their strategies, and identify threats and opportunities.

---

## Competitor Deep Dive

Build a comprehensive profile of a competitor.

```prompt
---
title: Competitor Report
description: Create a comprehensive competitive intelligence report
icon: file-search
---
Create a competitive intelligence report on {{Competitor|Competitor X}}:
- Company overview (funding, headcount, growth trajectory)
- Product features and pricing
- Target market and positioning
- Recent product launches and announcements
- Key executives and their backgrounds
- Customer reviews and sentiment
```

---

## Track Competitor Hiring Patterns

Understand competitor priorities through their job postings.

```prompt
---
title: Hiring Analysis
description: Analyze competitor hiring patterns to understand their priorities
icon: briefcase
---
Analyze what {{Competitor|Competitor X}} is hiring for. What roles are they prioritizing? What technologies are they using? What does this tell us about their product roadmap?
```

---

## Monitor Competitor Social Presence

Track what competitors are saying and how customers respond.

```prompt
---
title: Social Monitoring
description: Monitor a competitor's social presence and customer sentiment
icon: message-square
---
Monitor {{Competitor|Competitor X}}'s social presence:
- What are they posting about on X/Twitter?
- How are customers responding?
- What's the sentiment in Reddit discussions?
- Any notable praise or complaints?
```
