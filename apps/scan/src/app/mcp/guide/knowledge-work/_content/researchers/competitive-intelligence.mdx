# Competitive Intelligence

Monitor competitors, analyze their strategies, and identify threats and opportunities.

---

## Competitor Deep Dive

Build a comprehensive profile of a competitor.

```
Create a competitive intelligence report on Competitor X:
- Company overview (funding, headcount, growth trajectory)
- Product features and pricing
- Target market and positioning
- Recent product launches and announcements
- Key executives and their backgrounds
- Customer reviews and sentiment
```

**APIs Used:** Apollo Org Enrich → Firecrawl Scrape → Exa Search → Serper News → Reddit Search → Apollo People Search

---

## Track Competitor Hiring Patterns

Understand competitor priorities through their job postings.

```
Analyze what Competitor X is hiring for. What roles are they
prioritizing? What technologies are they using? What does this
tell us about their product roadmap?
```

**APIs Used:** Firecrawl Search (job boards) → Firecrawl Scrape (careers page) → Apollo People Search (recent hires)

---

## Monitor Competitor Social Presence

Track what competitors are saying and how customers respond.

```
Monitor Competitor X's social presence:
- What are they posting about on X/Twitter?
- How are customers responding?
- What's the sentiment in Reddit discussions?
- Any notable praise or complaints?
```

**APIs Used:** Grok User Posts → Grok X Search → Reddit Search → Reddit Post Comments

---

## Competitive Feature Comparison

Build a feature matrix across competitors.

```
Compare the feature sets of tools A, B, and C in the data
orchestration space. Create a matrix covering:
- Core features
- Integrations
- Pricing models
- Enterprise capabilities
```

**APIs Used:** Firecrawl Scrape (each product's docs/features pages) → Exa Search (comparison articles)
